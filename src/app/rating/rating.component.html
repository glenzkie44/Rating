Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League

Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League is a genre-bending action-adventure shooter video game set in an open world based on Metropolis. The game features four playable characters: Harley Quinn, Deadshot, Captain Boomerang, and King Shark. While it can be played solo, the game also features a four-player cooperative multiplayer mode. When played solo, players can switch between characters at will while the other characters are controlled by AI.
